Global Chat and Discord Rules

1. No Racial or sexually derogatory terms are allowed for any reason
2. No political shaming/insulting
3. No religious shaming/insulting
4.No offensive behavior is to be tolerated. this is subjective and I understand that and since you know that then you should know how to avoid it.

Main Game Rules

1. No cheating, under map raiding, boost raiding, garden plot raiding

2. No loot stacking! bags in bags, pouches in pouches etc etc

3. No loot cycling

4. No combat logging(timer set to 5 minutes)

5. No inside raiding/double agent/double faction or spying/logging inside enemy bases

6. No base call outs in public chats(unless you're the base owner)

7. KOS allowed everywhere besides safe zones- Traders and marked zones on the map

8. No claiming team mate bounties or killing a bounty on agreement to remove tracking. you can buy them off however

9. Green Mountain is a Safe Zone (500m), you can trade with other players here safely as well

10. No stealing at any safe zones, Green Mountain/Devils Castle

11. Do not build or place objects within city limits of the Traders

12. Faction leaders, you are the company you keep. don't recruit losers and cheaters. Their actions could cost you and your team a ban.

13. Nomads and Lone Wolfs. You are the company you keep as well and we see you sometimes run with other fellow loners. same applies to you. don't run with a loser or a cheater and get banned, it will not be fun.

14. No building near military zones. the red circles with lines indicate this

15. Be decent and use your head, this list will naturally get longer/change over time because people will argue ''there wasn't a rule for that'' we want a fun environment that most people can enjoy. So lets have fun!

Base Building and Base Raiding Rules

1. Be kind in the discord. kill each other in the game

2. No item stacking or fire stacking allowed. any other form of raiding that doesn't involve blowing down doors is considered illegal

3. You can build a 5x5x4 size base max

4. Doors must be raid-able, do not build them backwards/stack them or an admin will remove them

5. You can have a max of 15 doors, evenly spaced right now, this may change depending on the new economy next wipe

6. Any exploits that make base impossible to raid are not allowed

7. No building near military or points of interest

8. Raiding is allowed at all times

9. no glitch raiding allowed, you can use a vehicle to jump over a wall but any other form of raiding that doesn't involve blowing down doors is considered illegal

10. Try not to leave a mess, it will cause lag with items all over the ground. Bring a trash can for unwanted loot or leave in container

11. Once you raid a group don't raid them again the next day. wait at least a week between raids with the same group.

12. These rules may change up a bit, we are a growing community after all. just don't whine please, call for a vote.
